Health AI for All Network (HAINet)

HAINet is in its early stages and is actively building its community. We welcome individuals and organizations passionate about advancing equitable Health AI to join us and help shape the network’s vision and impact.

About HAINet

The Health AI for All Network (HAINet) is a growing network of individuals and organizations dedicated to developing, implementing, and promoting artificial intelligence technologies to improve health outcomes—particularly for people in resource-limited settings. HAINet’s mission is to:

  • Build a global network and ecosystem to advance Health AI that benefits all of humanity.
  • Create a shared global agenda to advance Health AI to help improve health outcomes for all people across the world.
  • Foster collaborative global action on Health AI to address pressing health challenges equitably.
  • Advance the Network's goals by means of physical and digital media and content curation, knowledge brokerage, advocacy, and educational activities.

The Network is guided by an interim steering committee comprising individuals with expertise across medicine, public health, AI, computing and informatics, policy and law, and other allied fields. The committee reflects diverse geographies and disciplines, with a strong focus on representation from low- and middle-income countries. As we grow, we are seeking new members to join this leadership team and contribute their unique perspectives and expertise to advance Health AI for all.

Together, we aim to create an inclusive, collaborative, and impactful global initiative to harness the potential of AI for better health worldwide.
