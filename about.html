Health AI for All Network (HAINET)

About HAINet

The Health AI for All Network (HAINet) is a network of individuals and organizations working on developing, implementing, and promoting artificial intelligence technologies to improve people’s health—with a primary focus on improving the health of people in resource-limited settings. HAINet works to:

  • Build a global network and an ecosystem to advance Health AI that benefits all of humanity.
  • Create a shared global agenda to advance Health AI to help improve health outcomes for all people across the world.
  • Enable collaborative global action on Health AI for all.
  • Advance the Network's goals by means of physical and digital media and content curation, knowledge brokerage, advocacy, and educational activities.

HAINet is governed through a steering committee of individuals working in medicine and public health, AI, computing and informatics, policy and law, and allied areas. Steering committee members are elected to represent geographies and disciplines, largely based in low- and middle-income countries, and bring a depth and breadth of experience in advancing Health AI in resource-limited settings.
